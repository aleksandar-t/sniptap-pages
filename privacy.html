SnipTap respects your privacy.

What SnipTap stores

SnipTap stores your app data locally on your device, including:

  • snippets
  • categories
  • template values
  • clipboard history saved inside the app
  • app preferences and settings

Clipboard access

SnipTap may read clipboard content only when you use clipboard-related features inside the app, such as creating or saving snippets from copied text.

Import and export

If you use import or export features, the selected files are accessed only for that purpose.

Feedback and issue reports

If you use the feedback/report issue feature, your email app may open with pre-filled technical information such as:

  • app version
  • Android version
  • device model
  • current screen
  • recent non-sensitive diagnostics

This information is only included to help investigate issues.

Data sharing

SnipTap does not sell your personal data.

SnipTap does not automatically send your app data to us unless you choose to send feedback or support information yourself.

Accounts and cloud sync

SnipTap does not currently require an account and does not currently provide cloud sync.

Contact
